  1. Data may be disclosed to entities supporting the Controller, including hosting providers (LH.pl), analytics tools (Google LLC), marketing services (Google Ads, Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd.), email tools (Brevo/Sendinblue SAS), and IT infrastructure service providers.

5. Data Transfers Outside the EEA

  1. We use service providers based outside the EEA (e.g., Google LLC – USA). Data is transferred based on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission (Art. 46 GDPR) or other mechanisms compliant with the GDPR.
  2. You may request a copy of the safeguards used by contacting us (section 3).

6. Data Subject Rights

You have the right to: access your data; rectify it; delete it; restrict processing; data portability; object; withdraw consent at any time; lodge a complaint with the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (UODO).

7. Automated Decision-Making

We do not use profiling or other automated decision-making in the Service that produces legal effects.

8. Security Measures

We apply, among others: SSL/TLS encryption, access control systems, backups, security monitoring, and regular audits.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

  1. The Service uses cookies in accordance with Article 173 of the Telecommunications Law. Cookies are categorized as:
